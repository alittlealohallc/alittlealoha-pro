About

Kent Schaeffer

Founder  ·  A Little Aloha LLC  ·  Utah LLC  ·  Est. 2026

Over 25 years of hands-on experience in enterprise Linux/Unix systems, DNS/DHCP/IPAM infrastructure, software development, technical writing, graphic design, and calligraphy. Formerly with Wells Fargo, Goldman Sachs, Fidelity Investments, Five9, and Consolidated Edison. Available for remote engagements and local on-site work throughout Salt Lake County.

The technical philosophy is straightforward: simple solutions to complex problems, secure by default, and free from unnecessary dependence on platforms that monetize your data or your clients' privacy.
